How the Residency Guide Works

View Time Line Based Guide

Each lesson in this module will help guide you through the process of researching, applying, and matching with a residency program. To help you plan your journey, each lesson will also include a header (above) with the recommended time frame you complete this stage of your Road to Residency journey.
