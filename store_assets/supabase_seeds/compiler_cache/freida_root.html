OFFICIAL AMA DATABASE

13,000+ ACGME accredited programs, enriched by AMA guidance and expertise.

FREIDA™ is a free to use application that allows you to search for a residency or fellowship from more than 13,000 programs - all accredited by the Accreditation Council for Graduate Medical Education (ACGME).

FREIDA™ Member Dashboard

Join the AMA and get access to member only dashboard where you can save, rank, take notes, download, and compare programs.

Expanded Listing

Unlock complete residency and fellowship program insights, AMA members get full access to program details to make smarter, faster decisions.

FREIDA™ Road to Residency Guide

Online course to help you select, apply and get admitted to residency programs.

Membership makes a difference

When you're trying to land a residency or fellowship, every advantage helps. The AMA gives members that edge with access to member-only features within FREIDA™, the AMA Residency and Fellowship Database®.
