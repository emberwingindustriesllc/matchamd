## 3. Long Description
Matching into a US residency or fellowship program as an International Medical Graduate (IMG) is one of the most complex journeys in medicine. MatchaMD was built to make it possible — with the tools, programs, and guidance you need, all in one place.

Whether you graduated from India, Nigeria, the Philippines, Egypt, Pakistan, or anywhere else in the world, MatchaMD gives you a clear, step-by-step path to matching.

🏥 IMG-FRIENDLY PROGRAMS
───────────────────────────
Search and filter verified IMG-friendly residency and fellowship programs ranked by their IMG acceptance history. Filter by specialty, state, visa sponsorship, Step score requirements, and IMG-friendliness score.

📋 YOUR PERSONAL CHECKLIST
───────────────────────────
Track every step — ECFMG certification, USMLE Step 1 and 2 CK, OET Medicine, ERAS registration, Letters of Recommendation, Personal Statement, NRMP Match registration, and more.

───────────────────────────
Never miss a critical match deadline. MatchaMD tracks ERAS opening dates, NRMP registration windows, program rank order list deadlines, and Match Day.

## 13. App Review Notes
MatchaMD is an educational app for International Medical Graduates (IMGs/FMGs) navigating the US residency and fellowship application process. It provides program search, application checklists, deadline tracking, guides, and community features. No clinical advice is provided — all content is educational/informational. AI chat features provide general pathway guidance only and include a disclaimer that users should verify information with official sources (ECFMG, NRMP, ERAS).
