# Tablet Screenshot Capture Guide

## Required
- 1 screenshot
- Device: tablets with screen size >= 7"
- State: sign in, on a populated meaningful screen
- Suggest screens:
  - Dashboard with checklist progress
  - Programs list with filters visible
  - Community feed populated

## Save to
`store_assets/screenshots/screenshot_06_tablet.png`

## Naming
phone screenshots: `screenshot_01_find_img-friendly_programs.png`
tablet screenshots: `screenshot_06_tablet.png`
